About

An Islamic App in which we installing the ways of Sunnah which will be helpful for the youth who want to change their life style and want to live according to Sunnah.
Daar-ul-jannah apps has many features such as "Tasbeeh counter" to count your zikr and maintain the history, "Mosque locator" to find the mosque in karachi city to perform prayers,Qaza salah tracker,reciting,learning and memorizing the Duas, Prayer times shows the timing of each prayer,hijri calendar, prayer guidance help the user how to pray salah using text and by the help of images of each step, 99 names of Allah and Muhammad(P.B.U.H) and Qibla direction.

  • Prayer Manager

    A good opportunity for those who repent back and are looking forward to reform their prayers again.

  • Prayer Times

    It gives the prayer times of the cities of Pakistan. It also shows the prayer time of karachi on calendar too.

  • Mosque Locator

    It allows user to find the nearest Mosque to perform Salah.

  • Prayer Guidance

    Step by Step Salah guidance of Male and Female for instructing Muslims all over the world about how to effectively offer prayers along with all the essential information.

  • 99 Names

    User can read and listen the audio of 99 Names of Allah and Muhammad (P.B.U.H).

  • Tasbeeh Counter

    User can add and read tesbeeh, even you close unfortunately,the number of the Zikr will not erased. When you restart the application, the Zikr number will appear on the screen.
